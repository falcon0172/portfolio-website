ABOUT
ME

Marketing/Digital Marketing Specialist

It was a bright Sunday morning; I was sipping on my hot chocolate when I had this crazy idea of leaving my existing financial job and getting into the creative business…

 

 

I began my career as a marketer in 2018, after that there was no looking back. Even as mini Disha, I experienced television with hypnotic amazement because of the visuals, editing, and the story that every campaign conveyed (that of course, I understood very differently).

Obviously, marketing has evolved significantly, but the basic concept still remains the same- to be remembered and talked about. Hence, you need to create a product or an experience that is more human-focused and has a compelling story, to make something awesome and transparent so that it easily becomes a part of the market and yet stands out.

I have an almost obsessive passion with everything digital, social, creative, tech and food (as you will discover when you hire me!).

Marketing now is more fun and challenging than ever. So, if you are looking for someone who can think big, small and crazy ideas, create campaigns and content for a wide range of audiences regardless of where they are experiencing the brand/product, secure great partnerships, and collaborate & work with multiple teams ranging from product to finance to sales to design, then we’re a match made in the marketing heaven.

 

Bonus of working with me: Daily dose of jokes and prank ideas that will definitely make you chuckle.
